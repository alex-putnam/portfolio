ABOUT ME

Starting from an early age building my first PC at 13, and seeing the world wide web develop in front of me, I have always been passionate about technology. Who remembers the good old days of 56K modems? Today as we know it the web is bigger than ever, and is constantly evolving. As I grew up I fell in love with film, photography, and music. I dived deeper into that industry while attending Arizona State University and Scottsdale Community College for film/media production.

After college I pursued my artistic passion, and worked with teams to curate all types of events while also organizing photo/film shoots. At the same time, I was also working on the forefront of the cannabis industry in California for 10+ years. Worked diligently over the years to be able to manage facilities and complete administration responisblities to solve the day to day. It is all these skills of management and working with teams, that make me a great asset to any organization.

It was only natural that the worlds of technology, my artistic passion, and management experience would come together to become a software engineer!

Currently able to assist clients in HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. Contact me to help make your vision a reality!
