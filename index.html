ABOUT ME

Starting from an early age by building my first PC at 13, and seeing the world wide web (WWW) develop in front of me, I have always been passionate about technology. Who remembers the good old days of 56K modems? Today as we know it the WWW is bigger than ever; and is constantly evolving. As I grew up I fell in love with film, photography, and music. After college I pursued my artistic passion, and worked with teams to curate all types of events while also organizing photo/film shoots. While working on my passion, I have also been on the forfront of the cannabis industry for 10+ years. Working my way up all these years to finally managing facilities and doing complete administration duties. It is all these skills of management and working with teams, that make a great asset to any organization.

It was only natural that the worlds of technology, my artistic passion, and management experience would come together to become a software engineer!
